Medical and Services

The medical college has an attached 1500+ bedded super-specialty hospital which has served the healthcare needs of over 1 crore patients since 2005. SRM Medical College regularly features at the top of national rankings in the country. The medical college is also a centre for vibrant research adding numerous publications, clinical trials, and vaccine trials to its list every year.

Agriculture

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) established the Faculty of Agricultural Sciences at Main campus, Kattankulathur by starting B.Sc. (Hons.) Horticulture degree program in 2018 and B.Sc. (Hons.) Agriculture degree program in 2019 which was approved by the Board of Mangement. Later, in 2021 the Faculty of Agricultural Sciences was shifted to Baburayanpettai, Chengalpattu District and started functioning as SRM College of Agricultural Sciences (SRMCAS) in an area of 47.21 ha, which is 112 km from Chennai and 68 km from the Main campus SRMIST, Kattankulathur, by covering the urban, semi-urban, and rural areas.

About Us

Vellore Institute of Technology is one of the finest private universities in India. The university has four VIT campuses, located at Vellore, Chennai, AP-Amravati, and Bhopal. All four campuses provide a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes to its students. VIT University offers 51 undergraduate programmes, 32 postgraduate, 11 Integrated Programmes and Research Programmes. SRM Institute of Science and Technology is one of the top ranking universities in India with over 52,000 full time students and more than 3200 faculty across all the campuses – Kattankulathur, Ramapuram, Vadapalani Campus – all in and around Chennai, Tiruchirappalli (in TN), Modinagar (in UP) & Sonepat (in Haryana) – both of which are located near Delhi NCR, Amaravati (in AP), Gangtok (in Sikkim) – offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in six Faculties – Engineering & Technology, Management, Medicine & Health sciences, Science & Humanities, Law and Agricultural Sciences.
